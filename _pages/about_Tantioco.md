Toni Tantioco is a Biomedical Research Masters student at Wake Forest. He earned his Bachelors of Science in Biology at the Missouri University of Science and Technology in May of 2025. In his undergraduate studies, he did research with Dr. David Westenberg on the MST nuclear reactor microbiota where he mainly focused on PCR testing of liquid samples. He joined the Sprockett Lab in September 2025, where he is studying the gut microbiome and performing analysis on 16S rRNA sequencing using Rstudio. In his free time, he enjoys exploring nature by hiking, playing video games, or eating at a new restaurant.
